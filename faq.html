01: How does KGF handle car scrap recycling?

KGF has specialized processes for recycling cars, ensuring that every valuable component is recovered and recycled efficiently. We handle everything from dismantling to safe disposal, following strict safety and environmental standards.

02: What is the process for metal scrap recycling at KGF?

KGF follows a comprehensive process for metal scrap recycling, starting from collection, sorting, and processing to final recycling. We deal with various metals, including steel and aluminum, ensuring that all materials are processed efficiently and reused in the industry.

03: How does KGF manage industrial demolition projects?

KGF’s team of skilled professionals is trained to handle industrial demolition projects safely and efficiently. From planning and execution to debris removal and recycling, we ensure that all processes are carried out with the highest standards of safety and environmental care.
