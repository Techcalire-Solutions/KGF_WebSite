About KGF

Who We Are

KGF Scrap Trading & Recycling Co. is a 3rd Generation, family owned company with a solid history of innovation and expertise.

As a reliable and flexible partner, We are professionally equiped to handle all kinds of metal scraps in any variable quantity starting from samll loads to truck loads.

A proficient, skillful and safety trained workforce who are professionally qualified to administer the demolision and recycling process with technical expertise is the most valuable assets of KGF.
