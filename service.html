  • DOCUMENTS SHREDDING

    At KGF, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your sensitive information with our top-tier document shredding services. Our state-of-the-art shredding process ensures that your documents are thoroughly destroyed, safeguarding against data breaches and identity theft.   • CAR SCRAPPING

    At KGF, we pride ourselves on a streamlined and hassle-free car scrapping process, ensuring a seamless experience for our clients. Our team of experts is dedicated to dismantling each vehicle meticulously, recovering valuable materials and components to reduce waste. With a focus on sustainability, we transform end-of-life vehicles into a resource, contributing to a circular economy.



  • SITE CLEARNCE

    KGF offers comprehensive site clearance services, ensuring your project starts with a clean slate. Our experienced team is equipped with the latest machinery and tools, enabling efficient removal of debris, hazardous materials, and unwanted structures. We adhere to strict safety and environmental standards, recycling and disposing of materials responsibly to minimize impact on the environment.




  • DEMOLISION SERVICES

    At KGF, we provide comprehensive demolition services ensuring safe and efficient dismantling of structures. Our expertise and cutting-edge technology prioritize the safety of your site, delivering a clean slate for future development while adhering to environmental and safety standards.

    KGF offers reliable electronic waste management solutions, ensuring the secure and eco-friendly disposal of electronic devices.
